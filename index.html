what is this

Anima2B is an AI image model created by Circlestone Labs in collaboration with Comfy Org, trained on millions of anime and illustration images spanning approximately 59k credited artists. This tool lets you check whether your name appears in that artist index.

The model in question: huggingface.co/circlestone-labs/Anima

if your work was used
If your name appears in the list, there is unfortunately limited recourse at the moment. Copyright law around AI training data is unsettled and still being actively contested.

However, what you can do right now is document everything: screenshot the model page, save the artist index, note the date. Connecting with other artists in the same situation amplifies the conversation considerably. Check with legal specialists that focus on art copyright, and spread the word.

Organizations focused on artist rights, including Netbloc and others, have been collecting this kind of documentation. You are not navigating this alone.
